Shipping

As an australian business, we strive ship orders on the next business day, but sometimes ship after 2 business days (excluding public holidays and victorian public holidays) and standard shipping can take anywhere from 1-7 business days while the Australia Post express network vastly changes in terms of delivery time based on your location. All orders are send via tracked shipping to ensure your parcels are never lost or delayed.

Returns

Puzzle Galaxy appreciates its customers and knows that products can break or go missing in shipping from time to time, this is why we offer a 30 day return policy that lets you return the item for a full refund if the item is deemed faulty or un-usable. There can be however a restocking fee if the packaging or product itself is damaged or missing due to customer related faults. Please feel free to message us for further details or to ask any questions you have.

Payments

Payments are to be made via PayPal and orders will only be shipped when any payment has been cleared, if you are paying via PayPal this is usually instant.
